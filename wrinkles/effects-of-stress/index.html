Effects of Stress Reduction and Meditation on Skin

Mar 14th, 2019 By Xavier wrinkles

Ever saw your acne aggravated during a stressful phase? If you lead a stressful life, it starts to show up on your skin. Having a calm life with meditation and reading can contribute to reducing stress significantly. All these are interlinked with skincare and skin condition.

Psychodermatology is an emerging research field that relates psychological responses to skin and its condition. It links the effects of different emotions on the skin.

We will first establish the link between stress and skin condition. Then, we will discuss the effects of stress reduction on the skin by practicing meditation, reading and leading a calm life.

How does stress affect skincare?

During a stressful phase or period, the body releases the stress hormone called Cortisol. This hormone induces the skin glands, along with its other functions, to produce more oil. Oily secretions clog skin pores, resulting in aggravation of acne, skin breakout, bumps, and zits.

Therefore, stress affects skincare directly. A part of a healthy skin care regime is to lessen stress in your life. We will tell you some ways on how that can be done.

Effects of stress reduction on skincare

Actively working towards reducing stress can help your skin. This can be done by practicing meditation, reading and leading a calm life.

Following are some of the effects yielded by these practices:

Meditation

Meditation is one such practice that can help reduce stress. What goes on in your mind reflects on your face, so taking care of our thoughts is essentially important.

Meditation helps in doing the following:

  • Increased mindfulness; this technique raises your self-awareness. You are able to analyze your positive and negative emotions in a better light. So, you can identify the solutions to certain triggers when you are more self-aware.
  • More positive energy; meditation helps you to instill more positive energy by letting go of the negative. This develops a positive vibe, and not only affects you but people associated with you. A positive person emits positivity, affecting their surroundings in a healthy way.
  • Relaxes your nerves; meditation can help in reducing stress by relaxing and calming your nerves. This can help you in leading a calm life with a less stressful body.
  • Makes you feel more in control; when you recognize your sensibilities and emotions, mindfulness and self-awareness lead you to be more conscious and in control of your emotions. Thus, you can have control over your stress rather than the vice versa.

Reading

Reading has been proven to lessen stress more than any other activity. Research shows that more than two-thirds of stress is reduced by just a 6-minute reading activity.

Calm life

A calm life eventually reduces stress. Consciously focusing on reducing stress and keeping yourself calm lessens negative emotions. This helps in releasing “happy hormones” like endorphins, oxytocin, dopamine, and serotonin. They contribute to a sense of well-being, happiness, and motivation.

When you feel good, you look good. Meditation and leading a calm life, thus contributing to skincare and should be part of your lifestyle.
